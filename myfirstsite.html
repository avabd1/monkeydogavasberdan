THE DOG AND THE MONKEY

by ava sophia berdan

Once upon a time, there was a sad dog.

Fortunately, the dog had loving owners.

Unfortunately, the dog didn't get his daily treats.

Fortunately, the dog got lots of good pats on the back.

Unfortunately, he was very hungry.

Fortunately, he was properly fed.

Unfortunately, His owners didn't take him on his walk.

Fortunately, he was in the best shape of his life.
